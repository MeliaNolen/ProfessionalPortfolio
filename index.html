About Melia

Melia is from the small southern town of Shelby, North Carolina. She attended the University of North Carolina at Wilmington where she achieved a Bachelor of Science degree in Mathematics along with a Minor in Statistics. After college, she moved back to Shelby to teach high school math. Having no educational experience or student teaching, she found it to be a challenge but overcame it to achieve a successful year of teaching. It was not, however, intellectually challenging enough for her. While thinking about a career change, Melia knew that she loved to use her mathematical mind to solve problems, think analytically, and appreciate logic but she also didn't want to be bored. For her, that's where coding came in. Coding has all of those wonderful characteristics of math that Melia feels so comfortable around but with an outlet for creativity. Seeing the promise of the perfect career for her, Melia enrolled in the University of North Carolina at Charlotte Full Stack Coding Bootcamp. Three months later, she began living her dream as a full stack web developer. Learning so much in a short three months was tough but she is continuing her career knowing that she has all of the tools to always be improving on what she does know, and most importantly, learning new technologies and innovative ways to use them.
